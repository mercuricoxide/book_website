SHEA THOMAS has always been intrigued by the supernatural. As the son of a minister, he grew up in the churches of Harlem and saw God and the Spiritual Realm as fact. Though, as a young adult he began to gravitate towards the sciences. After studying physics at Columbia University, Shea was content with mankind’s understanding of the natural world, but he still dreamed about the supernatural. Could Religion and Science ever be reconciled? In seeking this answer, The Cycle series was born. When Shea is not traveling to various countries for research or obsessing over philosophy, he can be found in his apartment in New York City, binge-watching anime.
