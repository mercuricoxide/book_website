ECLIPSE

Book I of The Cycle

SHEA THOMAS


Just when she thought her life had become perfect, seventeen-year-old Chloe Alexandros discovers that she has a third arm.

The phantom arm lies within her chest and sometimes, of its own accord, reaches out and engulfs its victims in flames. Chloe would rather keep the arm a secret, but when it grabs her boyfriend, his blazing body slowly rises into the New York City sky—visible to onlookers and turning the world’s attention to the event.

Chloe goes into hiding. All she has is news reports, which detail flamed bodies above Tokyo and Venice also floating helplessly towards outer space—there are others awakening with strange powers.

Chloe must make a choice: Continue to cower in fear, or find the other wielders of this phantom arm—on a journey that will unravel the secrets of flesh and spirit, and reveal the entities governing her fate.
